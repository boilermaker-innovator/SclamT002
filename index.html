Full Job Description

About Schlam Cru: Schlam Cru specialises in providing skilled tradespersons for the repair and maintenance of heavy earthmoving equipment on mine sites across WA's Pilbara and Goldfields regions.

What's in it for you:

  • Up to $75/hr + Super paid on ALL hours worked
  • 8/6, 2/1, 2/2 rosters
  • Flights packages from your nearest capital city
  • Get paid for your time completing inductions and training
  • Employee referral bonuses
  • The industry's best PPE kits
  • A whole bunch of other perks

Required Experience:

You'll need to have experience with a range of heavy earthmoving equipment repairs, such as - dump truck trays, G.E.T replacements, grader and dozer blades, boom and stick repairs, air arc gouging, crack repairs, lancing, chassis and body maintenance.

Qualifications and Requirements:

  • Australian Boilermaker trade qualification
  • Drivers Licence: C class or higher
  • High Risk Work Licence: Forklift and EWP is beneficial
  • Working at Heights ticket
  • National Police Check
  • Australian Citizen or Permanent Residents ONLY
